Proximity

AT&T

AT&T.com

According to the law of proximity, you have to group related elements together to communicate that they form a whole. AT&T's Website uses images and buttons to form a group in the nav bar.

Visual Hierarchy

TARGET

Target.com

Visual Hierarchy teaches that there is an order in which the human eye perceives what it sees. Target made its "25% off now" button more visual for sales.

Fitt’s Law

CHEVY

Chevy.com

Fitt's Law is about the bigger an object and the closer it is, the easier it is to use. Chevy used the slide show bar with large print and clickable as well. Where it says “dependable” is large enough and in an easy place to see and visually appeal, along with all their other features.
